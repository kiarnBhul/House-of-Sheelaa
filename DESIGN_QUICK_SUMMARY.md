## What Changed

### **1. Consistent Background**
- **Before:** Pure white background, white cards looked disconnected
- **After:** Light grey background (`#FAF8F7`), everything feels cohesive

### **2. Fixed Layout Overflow**
- **Before:** "RenderFlex overflowed by 29 pixels" error
- **After:** Using `SingleChildScrollView` - no overflow errors

### **3. Cleaner Cards**
- **Before:** Gradient backgrounds, large shadows, busy look
- **After:** White cards, light borders, subtle shadows - clean & professional

### **4. Calendar Integration**
- **Before:** Calendar in white box on white page - looked disconnected
- **After:** White card on light grey background - looks integrated

### **5. Time Slots Layout**
- **Before:** Wrap layout caused overflow and poor spacing
- **After:** 3-column grid layout - clean and organized

### **6. Better Typography**
- **Before:** Mixed text sizes, inconsistent styling
- **After:** Clear hierarchy - headers, labels, values
